Our Goals

Good Earth Organisation is a passionate collective of individuals who share a common belief - that the well-being of our planet and future generations depends on our collective efforts to protect and cherish the natural world. We have been tirelessly working to inspire and empower individuals from all walks of life, awakening within them an appreciation for the beauty and significance of the natural world that surrounds us.

Mission

To inspire and empower individuals to foster a profound connection with nature and drive positive change in our relationship with the environment.

Read More

Vision

A world where every individual recognizes the intrinsic value of nature and takes action to safeguard its beauty and diversity for current and future generations.

Read More

Educational Programs

We organize nature camps, workshops, and webinars designed to educate individuals of all ages on topics concerning nature, biodiversity, and sustainable development.

Read More

Plantation Drives

We Initiate and execute plantation drives, including miyawaki forest projects and greening of various public spaces like hospitals, educational institutions, highways, electrical substations etc. to enhance green cover and mitigate environmental degradation.

Read More

Awareness Campaigns

We utilise the power of social media and social networks to educate and foster appreciation for nature.

Read More

Awards

We recognize and reward individuals and organizations making commendable contributions to environmental conservation and animal rescue efforts..

Research and Conservation

We support research initiatives and provide internships to students for understanding and preserving the natural world
